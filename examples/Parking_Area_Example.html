Dimension Parking Area Example

Drag the active dimension buttons (top row) to reorder them.
Drag a button to the parking strip (right of the "Group by" select) to remove it from grouping.
Drag a parked dimension back onto the active row to re-add it.
Check the checkbox on any parked dimension to show it as an extra column in the table.
Drag parked dimensions among themselves to set the column order.
